# Navigation

## Current Direction

The intended top-level navigation direction is:

- Home
- My Grow
- Explore
- Community
- Network
- Learn

## Current Application Mapping

Some current routes still reflect earlier product structure and should be preserved until future consolidation work is explicitly planned.

- Home maps to the main overview experience.
- Sessions and Vault currently support the future My Grow pillar.
- Explore currently contains Source Explorer.
- Community contains shared grow activity and community insights.
- Network supports grower relationships and discovery.
- Learn contains education and tutorials.

## Future My Grow

My Grow is the future consolidation of:

- Sessions
- Active sessions
- Seed Vault
- Analytics
- Grow history

The goal is to make personal grow management feel like one connected workspace rather than several separate destinations.

## Explore

Explore answers: What should I grow?

Current and future Explore tabs:

- Sources
- Seeds
- Breeders (future)
- Genetics (future)

## Explore Relationships

- Sources connect growers to source-level performance, trust signals, and Source Reports.
- Seeds will connect growers to variety-level performance, grow outcomes, community photos, and source availability.
- Breeders will connect source reputation, genetics, and community outcomes.
- Genetics will connect strains, varieties, lineage, traits, and observed grow performance.

## Page Relationships

- Home summarizes what is happening across the user's account and the wider product.
- My Grow owns the user's personal grow activity and seed inventory.
- Explore turns community and product data into decision support.
- Community shows public grow experiences and aggregate trends.
- Network connects users with relevant growers and trusted signals.
- Learn helps users improve through structured education.

## Navigation Principles

- Keep top-level navigation stable.
- Preserve existing routes and deep links during transitions.
- Use labels that reflect user intent.
- Avoid adding top-level items when a tab or subpage is enough.
- Consolidate related experiences only when the replacement is fully ready.
