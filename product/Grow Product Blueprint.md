# Grow Product Blueprint

## Mission

Knowledge grows here.

## Purpose

Helping growers make informed decisions through community-powered intelligence.

## Vision

Become the operating system for growers.

## Core Product Pillars

- My Grow
- Explore
- Community
- Network
- Learn

## Core Principles

- Never break a working experience.
- Build alongside before replacing.
- Preserve existing functionality.
- Every sprint must leave the app demo-ready.
- Favor reusable components.
- Design for long-term scalability.
- Everything connects.

## Product Direction

Grow should help people understand what is happening in their own grows, what is working across the community, and what decisions are most likely to improve future outcomes.

The product should feel practical, trustworthy, and intelligent. Data should be easy to scan, but never stripped of context. Community insight should feel useful, not noisy. Every product surface should help growers know what to do next.

## Development Posture

- Protect existing routes, data, and user workflows.
- Establish new product structure gradually.
- Use shared UI patterns where they reduce duplication.
- Keep documentation, navigation, and component language aligned.
- Treat demo readiness as a sprint requirement, not a final cleanup task.
