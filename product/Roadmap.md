# Roadmap

## Phase 1: Explore Foundation

Goal: Establish Explore as the product home for discovery and decision support.

- Rename the top-level Sources destination to Explore.
- Preserve Source Explorer and Source Reports.
- Add a Sources and Seeds segmented foundation.
- Introduce a premium Seed Explorer coming-soon state.
- Keep the app demo-ready.

## Phase 2: Component Library

Goal: Define and prepare reusable UI patterns for future product expansion.

- Standardize heroes, cards, metrics, badges, and report sections.
- Align shared components with the Design Bible.
- Reduce duplication where it is practical and safe.
- Preserve the appearance and behavior of working experiences.

## Phase 3: Seed Explorer

Goal: Build the first real Seeds experience inside Explore.

- Help growers evaluate seed performance.
- Connect seed data to sources, community outcomes, photos, and confidence signals.
- Make seed-level discovery useful without overwhelming users.
- Preserve existing Source Explorer behavior.

## Phase 4: Community

Goal: Strengthen Community as the place to understand real grower experience.

- Improve discovery of public grow activity.
- Connect community snapshots to insights and Explore surfaces.
- Clarify public-safe data boundaries.
- Make community evidence easier to scan and compare.

## Phase 5: Network

Goal: Make grower relationships useful for trust, discovery, and learning.

- Help users find relevant growers.
- Surface relationship context and community credibility.
- Connect network activity to Community, Explore, and Learn where appropriate.
- Keep social features focused on grow intelligence.

## Phase 6: Learn

Goal: Make Learn the improvement engine for growers.

- Organize education around practical grow questions.
- Connect tutorials to user context, community patterns, and product actions.
- Make learning pathways easier to follow.
- Support beginners without limiting advanced growers.

## Phase 7: My Grow

Goal: Consolidate personal grow management into one connected workspace.

- Bring Sessions, Vault, Analytics, and history into a coherent My Grow structure.
- Help users understand how their grows are performing.
- Connect personal data to learning, community insights, and Explore decisions.
- Preserve existing workflows until the new structure is fully ready.
