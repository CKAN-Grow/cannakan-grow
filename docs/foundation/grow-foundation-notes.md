# Grow Foundation Notes

A living collection of validated ideas, architectural observations, and future platform capabilities.

Foundation Notes allow Grow to preserve strong ideas without interrupting the current development milestone. A note may guide research or later implementation, but it does not authorize product, schema, analytics, or application changes by itself.

## Status legend

- **Idea** — worth preserving but not yet validated
- **Validated** — aligned with the Grow Foundation
- **Research** — requires additional product or technical investigation
- **Planned** — assigned to a future platform phase
- **In Development** — actively being implemented
- **Implemented** — completed and incorporated into Grow

## Reusable Foundation Note template

```markdown
# Foundation Note FN-000 — Title

**Status:** Idea
**Captured:** Month DD, YYYY
**Related areas:** Area, Engine, Experience

## Observation

Describe the durable product or architectural observation.

## Proposed capability

Describe the capability worth preserving without treating the note as implementation authorization.

## Why it matters

Explain the member and platform value.

## Foundation alignment

- Relevant Grow Foundation principle

## Related models, engines, and experiences

- Canonical model, engine, or experience

## Explicit non-goals

- Work that this note does not authorize

## Dependencies

- Required product, evidence, privacy, or technical foundation

## Open questions

- Question requiring future research or a decision

## Future implementation direction

- Compatibility, migration, security, and validation expectations

## Decision history

- YYYY-MM-DD — Status or decision and rationale
```
